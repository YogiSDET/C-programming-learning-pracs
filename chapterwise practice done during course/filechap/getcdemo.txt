Vedanta subsidiary ESL Steel Ltd. has hiked long steel prices by 
INR 1,500/t ($20.06/t) for Feb'22 sales sources informed SteelMint
The effective price for rebar (12-32mm) and wire rods (5.5-6mm) are at 
INR 59,000-59,500/t/t) on exy-Delhi basis excluding GST at 18%
Rise in raw material prices, especially coal
coupled with healthy demand has led to the price hike
